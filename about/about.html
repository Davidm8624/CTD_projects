About us:


Welcome to Chat-to.dev, a vibrant community where programmers of all levels come together to share knowledge, discuss coding, and collaborate on exciting projects. At Chat-to.dev, we believe in the power of community-driven learning and aim to provide a alternative platform where developers can connect, inspire, and grow.

Our mission is to foster a supportive environment for programmers, both beginners and experts, to exchange ideas, seek assistance, and collaborate on coding endeavors. We envision Chat-to.dev as a space where the passion for programming is celebrated, and everyone has the opportunity to enhance their skills.

  • Chat rooms

    • Chats are organized by their language, framework or platform. Find others who share the same interest as you! If a chat in your area of interest is not avaliable please open one. Inactive chat rooms will be removed after a month if there is no demand. Chat owners will be able to regulate content and users in their room that do not follow the rules set by the room creator.

  • File sharing

    • Upload files from your local machine directly to chat to collaborate with others in real time. Share code, wireframes, or other project related content.

  • MarkDown support

    • Chats, Post, & comments all support .md format. Share links, code snippets or format your messages in a organized and readable manner inline.

  • Edit functionality

    • Need to make a quick change or fix some syntax? Use the editing tool to correct already submitted messages post & comments.

  • Engaging Post

    • Wana share instructions or a guide you made? Wana ask a question thats not chat room specific? Got some other long-form content that you want to distribute? Use the post feature. Any content submitted here is able to be viewed by anyone regardless of their chat-toom participation. Newest post will be shown on the hope page!

  • Hand shakes

    • If a post, comment, or trend is helpful or informative, give out a hand shake to share your appreciation and to help promote that content and author.

  • Karma

    • As you participate in the community by creating well liked post, productive comments, and helping others in need, you will accumulate Karma. Once you reach certian thresholds you will unlock more features such as trends. Your Karma shows how helpful you are to others and you should be respected for your contributions.

  • Newest Trends

    • Want to share an article you read or some news you saw. Make a trend! Trends will display images as the main content and will host a link pointing to the article you are sharing. To unlock trends participate in the community and earn Karma. Trends will show on the home page.

  • Community driven environment

    • Everyone here is looking to share or learn. Embrace the spirit of collaboration and engage with others to further everyone! We hope to have you here and grow together.

  • Inclusive Community

    • We welcome developers of all backgrounds and experience levels. Whether you're just starting or have years of experience you have a home here. We are not a language/framework specific fourm, we allow all development related content.

  • Respectful Environment

    • We want to maintain a respectful and open space. Harrassment or discrimination are not tolerated as we strive to maintain a positive atmosphere. Our moderation team and chat room owners will enforce community guidelines to ensure a save environment. For more details about what is not permitted please read Terms of use.
